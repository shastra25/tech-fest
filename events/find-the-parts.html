Find The Parts

Event Timing

12:00 PM

Rules and Regulations

  • Participants are shown a photo or image for a limited time.
  • Each photo has one or more missing parts.
  • Participants must identify and write/draw the missing parts correctly.
  • No mobile phones, internet, or external help is allowed.
  • Points are awarded for accuracy and speed.
  • The participant/team with the highest points at the end wins.
  • Time limit for each photo/image/logo display is 10 seconds.
  • Submit the answers within the given time.
  • Disqualified for incorrect submissions or rule violations.
  • In case of any disputes, the organizers' decision is final.
