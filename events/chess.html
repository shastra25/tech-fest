Rules and Regulations

  • Participants: Individual players only.
  • Game Format: Standard chess rules on a regular chessboard.
  • Time Limit: Each player gets a fixed time (10–15 minutes). Use a chess clock.
  • Moves: Touch-move rule applies. Move only on your turn.
  • Winning:
    • Checkmate = Win
    • Opponent’s time runs out = Win
    • Draws possible by stalemate, threefold repetition, 50-move rule, or mutual agreement
  • Scoring: Win = 1, Draw = 0.5, Loss = 0
  • Tie-Breakers: Opponent scores, direct encounter, or number of wins
  • Fair Play: No cheating, electronic devices, or outside help
  • Decision: Organizer’s/arbiter’s decision is final
