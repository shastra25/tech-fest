Chart Presentation

Event Timing

Rules and Regulations

  • Eligibility: Participants can register individually or in teams of 2 (if applicable).
  • Paper Guidelines:
    • The paper must be original work of the participant(s).
    • Topics should be related to the theme/field of the competition.
    • Title Page should include Title, Name, Institution, and Contact.
  • Presentation Rules: Presentation must be made on chart papers.
  • General Rules:
    • Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
    • Use of offensive or inappropriate content is strictly prohibited.
    • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
    • There is one hour time for the preparation of the chart.
  • Registration Fee: ₹50/-
