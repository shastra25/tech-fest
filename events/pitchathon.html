Pitchathon

Event Timing

2:30 PM – 4:00 PM

Rules and Regulations

  • Participants can register individually or in a team of 2.
  • Each team can only submit one idea.
  • Ideas should aim to solve a real-world problem or present an innovative solution.
  • Each team gets 10 minutes to present their pitch.
  • Teams may use slides, demos, or prototypes.
  • In case of disputes, the organizers’ decision is final.
  • Judging Criteria: Innovation, feasibility, and impact.
  • Registration Fee: ₹50/-
  • Prize Pool: ₹1000/-
